### Q4: How often should I update my coaching website to maintain its SEO ranking?

Frequency matters, but quality is paramount. In the Kota context, your site should be updated at least twice a week. This could be a new blog post about "Preparation Tips for NEET," an update on batch timings, or a "Student of the Month" feature. Regular updates signal to search engine crawlers that your site is active, which encourages more frequent "recrawling." This ensures that when you announce a new batch, it shows up in search results almost immediately.

### Q5: Is it better to host demo videos on my site or embed them from YouTube?

Technically, you should always embed from YouTube or Vimeo. Hosting video files directly on your free website server will consume excessive bandwidth and significantly slow down your page load times, which will hurt your SEO. By embedding, you offload the heavy lifting to YouTube’s servers while also benefiting from "Video Search" rankings. Ensure you use the `lite-youtube-embed` technique if possible to prevent the YouTube player from loading until the user clicks, saving vital milliseconds in initial page load speed.

### Q6: What is the most important mobile optimization for Kota students?

The "Thumb Zone" design is the most critical UX factor. Most students will be browsing your site while commuting or during short breaks. Ensure that your primary "Contact" or "Enroll Now" buttons are within easy reach of a user's thumb. Furthermore, ensure that your font size is at least 16px to prevent "tap target" errors in Google Search Console, which can negatively impact your mobile usability score and, subsequently, your ranking.
